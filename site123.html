About

As a voice-over professional living in Doha, Omar offers many different types of media projects. His voice has a wide range, and can become just the sound you’re looking for. Let's work together!

Omar offers voice-over services such as: Commercials, Promos, Documentaries, Telephone on-hold (IVR), Animation, Corporate or industrial presentations, podcasts & educational videos.
